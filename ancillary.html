Ancillary

SourceWise offers a comprehensive range of ancillary services to complement our core machining and fabrication capabilities. These services include wire cut & EDM, laser cutting, water jet cutting, casting & etching, and optical profile grinding. Each of these services plays a crucial role in enhancing the quality, precision, and versatility of the components we produce.
